A Little About Me

I'm a freelance mechanical engineer and CAD designer living in New Hampshire, USA. I started freelancing in 2013 and have worked in a wide range of industries. Primarily, I have worked in the energy industry, while recently I have been more focused in consumer products.

I offer a complete design solution for your project. I can take your initial concept from an idea to a complete production ready product. Conversely, I can take a project at any stage in its development and move it forward. I am extremely flexible, and am always looking for a challenge.
