","\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t

Develop a novel water filter than can work purely on gravity with a maximum of 3ft of head.

","\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t

Project Details

","\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t

There was a hole in the water filtration market for a cheap filter that could work purely on flow supplied by gravity for individuals."," Existing products on the market generally require a pump to force the water through the filter with an acceptable flow rate. "," The reason a pump is needed is because of the cylindrical shape of a typical filter cartridge. For this product, a flat configuration was chosen which increased "," the surface area, and allowed it to generate a high flow rate with minimal head. The other innovation was a clamping and sealing method that allowed the filter to "," be assembled with only two plastic parts.","

Develop a novel water filter than can work purely on gravity with a maximum of 3ft of head.

","\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t

Project Details

","\t\t\t\t\t\t\t\t

There was a hole in the water filtration market for a cheap filter that could work purely on flow supplied by gravity for individuals."," Existing products on the market generally require a pump to force the water through the filter with an acceptable flow rate. "," The reason a pump is needed is because of the cylindrical shape of a typical filter cartridge. For this product, a flat configuration was chosen which increased "," the surface area, and allowed it to generate a high flow rate with minimal head. The other innovation was a clamping and sealing method that allowed the filter to "," be assembled with only two plastic parts.","
